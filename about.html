What is Verda?

Verda is a virtual storefront delivery service, linking users to a variety of dispensaries intent on providing the best selection of products ranging from classic to high end cannabis products, delivered to your location within a few hours.

Aimed at creating a channel for customers of the newly legalized cannabis market to have a quick, easy, and convenient way to order goods, Verda’s base launch will be in Manitoba, August 2018, but will expand services across Canada within the next 2 years.

What is our vision?

Verda’s goal is to dissolve the stigma, and change the way people perceive cannabis through advocating for extensive, thorough research on its medicinal benefits. Verda plans on becoming a donor for medical cannabis research with funds raised through our merchandise campaign.

Together, we can end the stigma and #BreakTheNorm
